Lasagna


Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6.5 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 cans (6 oz) tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1½ teaspoons dried basil
  • 1½ teaspoons salt (or to taste), divided
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 450 g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 340 g sliced mozzarella
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients and equipment.
  2. In a large pot, cook Italian sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water, sugar, herbs, and seasonings. Simmer the sauce for 1½ hours.
  4. Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente, then rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with an egg, parsley, and salt to make the cheese layer.
  6. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Assemble the lasagna: layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, more sauce, and Parmesan. Repeat layers.
  8. Cover with foil (spray it with cooking spray or keep it from touching the cheese) and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
